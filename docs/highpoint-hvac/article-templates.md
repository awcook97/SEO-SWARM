# HighPoint HVAC article templates

These templates support local SEO content such as service-area landing articles and topical HVAC guides. Use only approved inputs for business facts, service areas, pricing, and guarantees.
Schema is required on every page (include the relevant structured data for the template).

## Service-area landing article template

Purpose: target a specific city or service area with a localized service article that complements a landing page.

### Inputs

- Primary service keyword: [Primary Service]
- City or area: [City or Area]
- Secondary keywords: [Secondary Keywords]
- Approved proof points: [Proof Points With Sources]
- Service area list (verified): [Service Areas]
- Internal links: [Service Page], [Contact Page], [Related Services]

### Structure

1) Title + intro
- H1: [Primary Service] in [City or Area]
- 2 to 3 sentence intro with local context (approved only).

2) What this service covers
- 2 to 3 short paragraphs plus bullet list of scope.

3) Local considerations
- Climate, housing, or system context only if verified.
- Mention neighborhoods or nearby landmarks from approved list.

4) Common issues in [City or Area]
- 5 to 7 symptom bullets tied to the service.

5) Why HighPoint HVAC
- 3 to 5 proof points with sources.

6) FAQs
- 5 to 7 FAQs based on service briefs.

7) Call to action
- Primary CTA + approved phone number and hours.

### Metadata and schema

- Title tag: [Primary Service] in [City] | HighPoint HVAC
- Meta description: [Primary Service] in [City]. [Proof Point]. Call [Phone].
- Schema (required): LocalBusiness + Service, FAQPage if FAQs included.

## Topical service guide template

Purpose: evergreen educational content for HVAC topics (maintenance, troubleshooting, efficiency).

### Inputs

- Topic: [Topic]
- Primary keyword: [Primary Keyword]
- Secondary keywords: [Secondary Keywords]
- Approved sources: [Sources]
- Internal links: [Relevant Service Page], [Contact Page]

### Structure

1) Title + intro
- H1: [Topic] for Homeowners
- 2 to 3 sentence overview and who the guide is for.

2) Key takeaways
- 3 to 5 bullet summary.

3) Core sections
- Section A: explanation of the concept.
- Section B: steps or checklist.
- Section C: common mistakes and how to avoid them.

4) When to call a pro
- Symptoms that indicate professional service.
- Link to relevant service page.

5) FAQs
- 4 to 6 FAQs from approved sources.

6) Call to action
- Primary CTA + approved phone number and hours.

### Metadata and schema

- Title tag: [Topic] | HighPoint HVAC
- Meta description: [Topic] guide with tips from HighPoint HVAC. Call [Phone].
- Schema (required): Article, FAQPage if FAQs included.

## Compliance notes

- Do not include medical, legal, or safety claims beyond verified sources.
- Avoid “best,” “top-rated,” or “#1” claims without third-party proof.
- Keep local references constrained to verified service areas.
