# HighPoint HVAC webpage templates

These templates are for local SEO landing pages and service pages. Use only approved inputs for business facts, service areas, pricing, and guarantees. Do not invent claims.
Schema is required on every page (include the relevant structured data for the template).

## Local landing page template

Purpose: rank for a service in a specific city or service area page.

### Page inputs

- Primary service keyword: [Primary Service]
- City or service area: [City or Area]
- Secondary keywords: [Secondary Keywords]
- Approved proof points: [Proof Points With Sources]
- Service area list (verified): [Service Areas]

### Page structure

1) Hero
- H1: [Primary Service] in [City or Area]
- Subhead: One sentence value prop based on approved proof points.
- Primary CTA: [Schedule Service] or [Call Now]
- Secondary CTA: [Get Estimate] or [Request Service]

2) Service overview
- 2 to 3 short paragraphs on what the service covers.
- Bullet list of problems solved.

3) Why HighPoint HVAC
- 3 to 5 proof points with sources.
- Licenses, certifications, or guarantees only if verified.

4) Local trust section
- Neighborhoods or nearby landmarks (approved list only).
- Short paragraph on local response times or availability (only if verified).

5) Process
- 4 to 6 step process: Diagnose, Recommend, Repair, Verify, Follow-up.

6) FAQs
- 5 to 7 FAQs based on service briefs.

7) CTA block
- Repeat primary CTA with phone number (approved).
- Hours and service window (approved).

### Metadata and structured data

- Title tag: [Primary Service] in [City] | HighPoint HVAC
- Meta description: [Primary Service] in [City]. [Proof Point]. Call [Phone].
- Schema (required): LocalBusiness + Service, FAQPage where appropriate.

### Internal linking

- Link to related services: [Related Service Links]
- Link to primary service hub: [Service Hub Link]
- Link to contact page: [Contact Link]

### Compliance notes

- All claims must cite sources.
- Avoid superlatives unless proven by third-party data.

## Service page template

Purpose: evergreen page for a core HVAC service (no city targeting unless needed).

### Page inputs

- Service name: [Service Name]
- Primary keyword: [Primary Keyword]
- Secondary keywords: [Secondary Keywords]
- Approved proof points: [Proof Points With Sources]
- Warranty or guarantees (if verified): [Warranty Details]

### Page structure

1) Hero
- H1: [Service Name]
- Subhead: One sentence value prop based on approved proof points.
- Primary CTA: [Schedule Service] or [Call Now]

2) What this service includes
- 3 to 5 bullet points with scope.
- Optional: brands or systems served (only if verified).

3) Common signs you need this service
- 5 to 7 bullets focused on symptoms.

4) Why HighPoint HVAC
- 3 to 5 proof points with sources.
- Customer satisfaction statement only if verified.

5) Pricing and estimates
- Explain estimate process; avoid unapproved pricing.
- If pricing ranges are allowed, include source and date.

6) Service process
- 4 to 6 steps with clear expectations.

7) FAQs
- 5 to 8 FAQs based on service briefs.

8) CTA block
- Repeat primary CTA with phone number (approved).
- Emergency or after-hours messaging only if verified.

### Metadata and structured data

- Title tag: [Service Name] | HighPoint HVAC
- Meta description: [Service Name] with HighPoint HVAC. [Proof Point]. Call [Phone].
- Schema (required): Service, LocalBusiness, FAQPage where appropriate.

### Internal linking

- Link to related services: [Related Service Links]
- Link to service-area pages: [Service Area Links]
- Link to maintenance plans: [Maintenance Link]

### Optional modules

- Before/after or project highlights (only with approved assets).
